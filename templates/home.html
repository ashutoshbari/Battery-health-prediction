📚 About Battery Health

Battery health refers to how well your device's battery can hold and deliver charge compared to when it was new. Over time, lithium-ion batteries naturally degrade due to factors like usage, heat, charge cycles, and charging habits.

💡 Good Practices to Maintain Battery Health:

  • ✅ Avoid charging to 100% or draining to 0% frequently.
  • ✅ Keep your device in moderate temperatures (ideally 20–30°C).
  • ✅ Use original or certified chargers to avoid voltage spikes.
  • ✅ Unplug once fully charged to prevent overcharging stress.
  • ✅ Avoid heavy multitasking while charging (heating impact).

Monitoring your battery's health regularly helps prolong its lifespan and ensures your device runs efficiently. Our app helps you track these parameters and gives an estimate based on real-world usage factors.
